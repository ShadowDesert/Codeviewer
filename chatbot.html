Codeviewer ChatBot

How to add Chat Bot into your website?

1. Add "<script src="assets/src/chatbot.js" defer></script>" to your website.

2. Visit chatbot key generator and generate a key pair for your chatbot.

3. Replace "CCBPassCode = "Your public key";" With your generated public key.


How to use Chatbot

1. Just tap on the chatbot icon to open chat bot.


How touse chatbot manager?

1. Add "<script src="assets/src/chatbot-manager.js" defer></script>" to your website.

3. Replace "CCBPassCode = "Your public key";" With your previously generated private key.
